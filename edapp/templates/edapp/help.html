If you wanted to make education cheaper in Canada specifically, you would need to contact the provincial governments of Canada; they control the post-secondary education costs. Although, it would be difficult for you to succeed in lowering the costs of education. You could try to contact companies and ask them to drop their degree requirement. This would make self-taught routes become more viable; that is a cheaper route.
