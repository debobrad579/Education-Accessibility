Lack of Accessible Education

Some people have plenty of intelligent potensial and creative ideas, but they can’t activate that potensial; they don’t have easy access to education. These people could solve real-world issues or work a better job than they do, but they are limited to the education resources provided to them, and the money they have. Public elementary/middle/high school is provided completely for free in Canada, but post-secondary education is a different story: It is extremely expensive. That is the problem: People can’t afford it.
